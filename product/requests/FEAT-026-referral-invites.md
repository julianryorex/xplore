## Problem

Invites (FEAT-003) drive growth but offer no incentive to share beyond social utility.

## Proposed solution

Reward organizers: e.g. 1 free AI credit per accepted invite (cap 5), or 1 week Plus extension when trip reaches 4+ members. Track referral attribution on invite links.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Invite links carry referrer UID
- [ ] Reward granted on first trip join by new user
- [ ] Fraud limits (same device, duplicate accounts)

## Dependencies

- FEAT-003, FEAT-021, FEAT-025
